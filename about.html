About Loksewa Prep

A student's passion project dedicated to making Loksewa preparation accessible to everyone

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create an inclusive platform where every aspiring civil servant has access to quality preparation materials, regardless of their economic background. We envision a Nepal where merit and dedication determine success, not financial resources.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, free, and regularly updated study materials for Loksewa examinations. We are committed to supporting students through their preparation journey with reliable resources, practice tests, and guidance.

About the Creator

Student at NIST College

Suyog Pandey is currently pursuing his studies at NIST College, Lainchaur, Kathmandu, with a passion for technology and education.

Passion for Helping Others

Driven by the belief that education should be accessible to all, Suyog created this platform to help fellow students succeed in their Loksewa journey.

Self-Taught Developer

Community Impact
