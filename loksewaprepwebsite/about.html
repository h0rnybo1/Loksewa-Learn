My Mission

As a student myself, I understand the challenges of accessing quality preparation materials for Loksewa examinations. My mission with Loksewa Prep is to provide a reliable source for Loksewa exam preparation that's completely free for everyone.

I believe that quality education and preparation resources should be accessible to all students and job seekers, regardless of their financial situation or location. Having experienced the struggle of finding good study materials firsthand, I decided to create this platform in 2025.

Through Loksewa Prep, I aim to build a supportive learning community where aspiring civil servants can find accurate, up-to-date, and comprehensive study materials without any cost barriers.

About Me

I'm Suyog Pandey, currently pursuing my studies at NIST College in Lainchaur, Kathmandu. As a student who has experienced the challenges of finding quality study materials for competitive exams, I was motivated to create something that could help others in similar situations.

This website is my personal project, born out of a desire to give back to the student community and help make Loksewa preparation more accessible. I'm passionate about education, technology, and public service, which led me to combine these interests into this platform.

While I'm still learning and growing as a student myself, I'm committed to continuously improving this platform and adding more valuable resources for fellow aspirants.

What We're Building

Comprehensive Study Materials

Well-organized and easy-to-understand study materials covering all subjects and topics in the Loksewa syllabus, created by experienced educators.

Practice Tests

A variety of mock tests designed to simulate the actual Loksewa examination experience, helping candidates assess their preparation level.

Previous Year Papers

Collection of previous year question papers with detailed solutions to help candidates understand the examination pattern and important topics.

Preparation Tips

Expert advice and strategies to help candidates prepare effectively for their Loksewa examinations and maximize their chances of success.

Updated Syllabus

The latest Loksewa examination syllabus for various positions, ensuring candidates are preparing with the most current information.

Community Support

A supportive community where candidates can connect, share resources, and learn from each other's experiences.

My Vision

My vision for Loksewa Prep is to create a platform that democratizes access to quality Loksewa preparation resources. As a student, I know how financial constraints can be a major barrier to pursuing career goals.

I envision Loksewa Prep as a comprehensive resource hub where students and candidates from all backgrounds can find everything they need to prepare effectively for their examinations, without having to spend money on expensive coaching or materials.

By providing free, high-quality resources, I hope to level the playing field and help talented individuals from all parts of Nepal realize their dream of joining the civil service and contributing to our nation's development.
