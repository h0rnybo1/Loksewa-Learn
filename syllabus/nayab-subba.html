Nayab Subba (Non-Technical) Syllabus

Comprehensive syllabus for the Nayab Subba examination.

Section 1: General Knowledge and Current Affairs

  • Nepalese Geography, History, and Culture
  • Current National and International Events
  • Constitution of Nepal
  • Human Rights and Fundamental Rights

Section 2: Office Management and General Accounting

  • Office Procedures and Documentation
  • Filing and Record Keeping
  • Basic Accounting Principles
  • Budgeting and Expenditure Control

Section 3: General Intelligence Test (GIT)

  • Verbal and Non-Verbal Reasoning
  • Logical Reasoning
  • Numerical Ability
  • Spatial Orientation
