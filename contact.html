Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to the platform?

We welcome contributions! You can share study materials, suggest improvements, or report errors. Contact us through any of the methods above.

Are all resources really free?

Yes! All our study materials, mock tests, and resources are completely free. This is a student initiative to help the community.

How often is content updated?

We regularly update our content based on the latest Loksewa patterns and user feedback. Major updates happen monthly.

Can I request specific study materials?

Use the contact form above with "Content Request" as the subject, and we'll do our best to add the materials you need.

How can I report technical issues?

Please use the contact form with "Bug Report" as the subject, or email us directly. Include details about the issue and your device/browser.

Do you offer personal tutoring?

Currently, we focus on providing free online resources. However, we're exploring options for community study groups and peer support.
